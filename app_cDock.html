cDock 3

Awesome Dock customization for macOS 10.10 and above.

  • Download trial
    • Buy Now

cDock 3 gives you full control over the Dock. There are tons of ways to customize the look and feel of the Dock. You can even enable some awesome hidden features. cDock is designed to make theming your Dock easy and robust on macOS 10.10 and up.

There are many ways to configure cDock as you wish. Give it a go and find out!

Installation



- Download the latest release of cDock
- Open downloaded zip file
- Run cDock
- Allow cDock to move itself to the Applications folder
- You may be required to disable System Integrity Protection, cDock will inform you but cannot automate this
- You may be required to update System Components, cDock will do this for you
- Theme away
