VPK OS X

A minimal app designed to convert Folders into VPK and vise-versa

  • Download

  • Github

Information:

  • A simple app of bash scripts that will convert Folders into vpk and visa versa. VPK is the archive format used in most Valve games.
  • Drop items onto the Application and vpk will then process the items, either converting folders to vpk format or extracting vpk files to folders.
  • Can handle multiple items at once.
  • Supports notification center on OSX 10.8 and up.
